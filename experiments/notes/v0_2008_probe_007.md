## Focused Assessment

### Did the 2008 claims become consistently more directly causal?

Partially.

Relative to `004`, yes:

- the obvious descriptive/snippet-like securitization wording did not return
- both surviving `007` claims are shaped as direct causal answers

Relative to `006`, not clearly better:

- `007` only produced 2 claims instead of 3
- it replaced the cleaner `Deregulation of the OTC derivatives market...` claim with a broader Glass-Steagall claim

### Did descriptive/snippet-like phrasing decrease again?

Yes.

The exact bad `004` patterns:

- `is more than just a capital markets innovation...`
- `provides an additional funding source...`

did not reappear in `007`.

### Did groundedness remain acceptable?

No, not at the `006` level.

- `006` had `groundedness=1.0`
- `007` dropped to `groundedness=0.5`
- verification fell from `supported=3` to `supported=1, insufficient=1`

So the new phrasing is cleaner than `004`, but the run regressed materially on support quality compared with `006` and the `004` baseline.

## Interpretation

This looks like a mixed result:

- real improvement in answer shape versus `004`
- but not a stable improvement versus `006`
- and a real regression in groundedness/support metrics

Most likely explanation:

- the new hardening is successfully suppressing descriptive mechanism phrasing
- but provider/search variance plus thinner evidence in this particular live run left the system with fewer claims and weaker support coverage

This does **not** look like a new architecture bug. It looks like v0 live variance plus a tradeoff: cleaner claim shape, but not yet stable enough on support quality.

## Overall Judgment

### Is the targeted synthesis-quality issue now more stable?

Not yet.

The direct-causal phrasing improved relative to `004`, but the stronger `006`
result did not repeat reliably enough to call this stable.

### Did groundedness/claim quality stay acceptable?

Only partially.

- claim phrasing quality improved over `004`
- but groundedness and verification regressed meaningfully versus both `004` and `006`

### Is this fix strong enough to justify rerunning the full 3-question set?

No.

The 2008 probe is still too unstable. It would be premature to rerun the full
Tier 1 set before tightening this last narrow 2008 synthesis tradeoff further.

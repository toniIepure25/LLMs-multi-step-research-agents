## Focused Assessment

### Did the 2008 claims stay directly causal?

Only partially.

- `Securitization contributed to the 2008 financial crisis.` is a clean direct causal claim.
- The deregulation claim regressed into:
  - `Financial deregulation (and the reluctance to introduce regulation on new financial products) was a significant factor in preparing the conditions.`

That is more causal than a pure definition, but it is not a clean direct answer to the question and does not explicitly name the target crisis.

### Did the over-specific unsupported phrasing disappear?

Yes.

The brittle `007` claim:

- `Securitization of subprime mortgages contributed to the 2008 financial crisis.`

did not return. The new probe generalized back to:

- `Securitization contributed to the 2008 financial crisis.`

So the support-preserving generalization worked on that exact failure mode.

### Did distinct supported mechanism claims remain present?

No, not reliably.

- `006` had 3 distinct mechanism claims
- `007` dropped to 2
- `008` stayed at 2

The stronger OTC-derivatives-style mechanism claim did not return, and claim
count did not recover.

### Did copied/snippet-like wording stay reduced?

Yes.

The bad `004` snippet-like securitization wording remained gone.

### Did groundedness recover?

No.

- `006`: `1.0`
- `007`: `0.5`
- `008`: `0.5`

The securitization claim was still marked `insufficient`, and overall support
quality did not recover.

## Interpretation

This is a mixed but still unsuccessful stabilization attempt.

Real improvement:

- the exact `007` over-specific unsupported securitization phrasing disappeared
- copied/snippet-like wording stayed reduced

But the main tradeoff was **not** resolved:

- groundedness stayed low
- claim count stayed collapsed at `2`
- the strong deregulation/OTC-derivatives claim shape did not recover
- the replacement deregulation claim is broader and less directly phrased than `006`

This suggests the latest fix solved one narrow phrasing problem, but the live
run is still too sensitive to provider/evidence variance to preserve both clean
phrasing and strong supported claim coverage.

## Overall Judgment

### Did the support-preserving fix resolve the targeted tradeoff?

No.

It removed the worst over-specific unsupported phrasing from `007`, but it did
not restore groundedness, claim count, or the stronger supported mechanism
claim set from `006`.

### Did groundedness/claim quality stay acceptable?

Only partially.

- claim shape is still better than `004`
- but groundedness/support quality remains worse than both `004` and `006`

### Is this fix strong enough to justify rerunning the full 3-question set?

No.

The 2008 probe is still not stable enough. The remaining issue is now very
narrow, but it still needs one more focused v0 quality pass before rerunning
the full Tier 1 set.

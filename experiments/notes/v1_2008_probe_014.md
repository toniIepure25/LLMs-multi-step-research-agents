Yes.

This is the key result:

- the weak securitization-support issue from the full-set `v1_tier1_eval_set_002` 2008 run did not repeat
- groundedness recovered and held at `1.0`

No major regression in support quality.

- the synthesis sentence also drifted oddly toward `Falsified mortgage documents...`

So support stability improved, but claim phrasing and mechanism mix are still a
little variable.
